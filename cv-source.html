Abstract: I teach machines to read the structure hidden in sequences. My research turns video, electricity grids, glucose traces, and proteins into symbolic graphs (de Bruijn graphs, borrowed from genomics) so that neural models can reason over them. Eleven works spanning Scientific Reports, IEEE Access, Machine Learning: Science and Technology, IEEE OAJPE, and an ICML 2025 workshop; first author on ten.

Index Terms: de Bruijn graphs · video understanding · time-series forecasting · self-supervised & federated learning · graph neural networks · representation learning · reinforcement learning · PyTorch
